The Microexpressions of Architecture Visualization of building aging data in areas with medium to high humidity based on infrared imaging and humidity sensing

Based on the current living environment, there are a large number of explicit or implicit housing aging phenomena. However, there is often an emotional connection between people and their living space. When conducting housing inspections, residents often have doubts about the inspection results and are difficult to accept. Therefore, I hope to provide better methods and visualization of housing aging inspection, so that residents can better understand their living environment.
